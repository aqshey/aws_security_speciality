CloudHSM:
- CloudHSM is a cloud-based hardware security module (HSM) that enables you to easily generate and use your
  own encryption keys on the AWS Cloud.
- CloudHSM provides secure key storage and management, as well as hardware-based encryption and decryption
    capabilities.



Integration with Aws and 3rd party services:

- CloudHSM can be integrated with AWS services, such as Amazon RDS, Amazon Redshift, and Amazon S3, to
    provide secure key storage and encryption for your data.
- CloudHSM can also be integrated with third-party applications and services using the PKCS#11 and Java
    Cryptography Extension (JCE) interfaces.
- CloudHSM provides APIs and SDKs that allow you to easily integrate your applications with the CloudHSM
    service.
- CloudHSM is a fully managed service that handles the deployment, management, and maintenance of the HSMs,
    allowing you to focus on building secure applications.
- CloudHSM is compliant with industry standards, such as FIPS 140-2 Level 3, and provides secure key storage
    and encryption capabilities for your data.
- CloudHSM is a cost-effective solution for protecting sensitive data and meeting compliance requirements,
    such as PCI DSS, HIPAA, and GDPR.
- CloudHSM is available in multiple regions and availability zones, allowing you to deploy HSMs close to your
    applications and data for low latency and high availability.
